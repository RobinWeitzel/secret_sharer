Secure Secret Storage Across Two Locations

Secret Sharer helps you securely store sensitive information like passwords, recovery codes, or important documents by splitting them across two physical locations.

Use Case: Leave passwords to your accounts with family members in case you're unavailable or incapacitated. Without both parts, the information remains secure.

How It Works

1

Encrypt Your Secrets

Enter your sensitive information and generate two QR codes: one contains encrypted data, the other contains the decryption key.

2

Print and Store

Print both QR codes on separate pages with instructions. Store one at your location and another at a trusted location.

3

Decrypt When Needed

Scan one QR code, then the other. The secrets are decrypted entirely in your browser with no data sent to any server.

Security Features

  • State-of-the-art symmetric encryption
  • 100% client-side processing - no backend, no data transmission
  • Split-key protection - both QR codes required to decrypt
  • Open source and transparent
